Super Powers

After my recovery, I began to notice strange things happening when I was around water.
In the bath, going swimming, even drinking a glass of water.
It was like the water had a mind of its own, I can’t explain it.
The water in my glass would


swirl


up and down.
Until my teenage years it was manageable, and didn’t cause too much of a mess.
When I turned thirteen, things changed.
My glass would spill, the shower would have uncontrollable pressure,
the bath would
flood





















I didn’t know why. I was overwhelmed.
So I decided to run away.
I needed to be somewhere that I could be far enough from possibly hurting anyone with this strange power.
